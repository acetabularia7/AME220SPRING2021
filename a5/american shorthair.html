About the American Shorthair

American shorthair cats are known for their longevity, health, beauty, and sweet personality. The American shorthair breed originated from settlements from Europe to North America. An impure shorthair cat may look like an American shorthair cat, just as another randomly bred cat looks like a Siamese, Persian, or Maine Coon. However, the difference is that purebred cats can consistently raise kittens with the same body shape, coat quality and temperament, while cats raised randomly cannot. American shorthair cats usually continue to grow until they are 3 or 4 years old, and usually only require vaccinations and veterinary inspections once a year. Through a good diet and a lot of tender care, they can live for 15 years or more.
